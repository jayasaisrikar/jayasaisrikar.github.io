STEPS TO CANCEL TICKET



  1. Fill PnrNo and Transaction Id properly
  2. Fill the Ticket Cancellation form with correct deatils
  3. Next click on the proceed button to cancel ticket
  4. Then a new page opens i.e reason for cancelling ticket
  5. Now select the reason for cancelling
  6. Click on the submit button
  7. You see a message thank your for cancelling ticket
  8. The process for cancelling ticket is done
